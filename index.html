Buiding Excellence in Young Minds

Welcome to Bright Child Academy

You are warmly welcome to Bright Child Academy. This is a vibrant and growth focussed Academy that helps every child grow individually and at their own pace also making them ready for life stages and challenges with a key factor of high standard and passion.

Whether a student starts with in Beginning, Lower, Middle, or Upper School, our program steadily builds students’ intellectual prowess and resilient character, ensuring that each continually strengthens the other. When students reach our Upper School, they stretch themselves even further as they look ahead to the next phase of their lives.

Bright Child Academy provides an excellent support structure for students in terms of information, advice and guidance on further study and careers. We work closely with a number of different businesses on different projects over the academic year, and also try to get as many businesses/individuals to come and speak in lessons too.

We work with a range of careers adivsers. Students receiev support during the personal development lessons, tutor time and set activities during personal development days.
